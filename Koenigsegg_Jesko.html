Overview on Koenigsegg Jesko

Engine

This vehicle is one wild and impresive vehicle and my personal favorate. First we will look at the heart of this beast. The Jesko features a twin turbocharged 5.0-leter V8 gernerating 1280 horsepower and 1106 lb-ft of torque on pump gas. On E85, the output jumps up to around 1600 horsepower. The Koenigsegg Jesko vehicle has been claimed it's engine the lightest V8 ever made.

Transmission

With my brain overloaded with power lets move on to the gearbox of this vehicle. The Jesko comes with an amazing nine-speed gearbox and is also very light and reffered to as the Light Speed Transmission or LST for short. Built by Koenigsegg, the LST features a dual-clutch.

Performance

The Jesko also comes with an exclusive rear-wheel-steering system witch heightens performance. This improves steering ability and boost stability. The last major factor in this section is the downforce. The Jesko can produce around 1760-pounds of downforce at 250 kph(155 mph) and around 3100-pounds at higher speeds along with around 3150-pound curb weight. Overall the power and performance are just amazing to read but i never doubted this amazing vehicle.

Interior

Intering the Jesko you will first approach the ingress and egress doors. Next you with sight the leather, carbon, and aluminum. Once your inside you should also see the crome finished aluminum door handles. Looking up the frame of the door youll see an audio system built into the carbon panel along with a hands free microphone. If your wondering where the keys will be kept, worry not for there is a magnetic key holder right of the steering wheel. Talking about the steering wheel, there are controllable options to better help the driver with simple and menu task. Moving right again are the easly adjustible air vents along with a tablet display that includes A two seat interior with adjustible pedals and a detachable hardtop for your beachside cruses. Along with the pedals there is also an adjustible steering column
